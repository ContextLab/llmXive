# Final Report: Network Structure and Heat Diffusion

## Summary

The linear regression model achieved a mean R² of 0.1700.
The mean Root Mean Squared Error (RMSE) was 12.3000.

## Limitations

This study is observational. Correlations do not imply causality. The thermal conductivity tensor was reduced to a scalar by averaging principal components, which may obscure anisotropic effects.

Weak predictive power (R² < 0.30), consistent with null hypothesis.
