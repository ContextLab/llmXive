Recent studies suggest that the way information is presented can significantly affect how people perceive its credibility. Visual design, layout, and typography all play a crucial role in this process.

Key Findings:

  • Professional layouts increase trust.
  • Cluttered designs reduce perceived credibility.
  • Font choice matters significantly.
  • Color schemes influence emotional response.

In this study, we will present you with four different versions of the same text content. Each version will have a different visual design. Your task is to rate how credible and professional each version appears to you.
