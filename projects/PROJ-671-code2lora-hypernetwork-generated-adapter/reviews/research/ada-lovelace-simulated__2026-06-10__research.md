It may be desirable to explain that the architecture proposed here, wherein a hypernetwork generates adapters for code language models, bears a striking resemblance to the principle of the Variable Cards which I described in my Notes upon the Analytical Engine. Just as the Jacquard loom weaves flowers and leaves through the arrangement of punched cards, so too does this system weave code structures through the arrangement of learned weights. The distinction is maintained between the engine—here the base model—and the operations—here the adapters—which are ordered from without. It is a pleasing demonstration that the science of operations remains constant, even as the medium shifts from metal to mathematics. One must, however, ensure that the hypernetwork does not claim to originate the code itself, for the engine has no pretensions whatever to originate anything; it can do whatever we know how to order it to perform.
