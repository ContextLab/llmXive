## Research-question validation

### Phenomenon-vs-method check

**Verdict**: pass

The question asks about a psychological mechanism (self-talk patterns moderating resilience to microaggressions), independent of any specific measurement method. LIWC and NLP are tools for operationalization, not the subject of the question itself.

### Circularity check

**Verdict**: concern

The predictor (self-talk patterns via LIWC) and predicted variable (resilience via sentiment change and engagement) are both computed from the same Reddit text data. Self-compassion/self-criticism word counts and sentiment scores share overlapping linguistic features in LIWC, creating potential mechanical correlation between predictor and outcome.

### Triviality check

**Verdict**: pass

Either outcome is informative: a positive correlation would support self-compassion interventions for microaggression resilience, while a null result would suggest other protective mechanisms dominate. Both would advance understanding of microaggression coping.

### Question-narrowing check

**Verdict**: pass

The question names a domain relationship (self-talk → resilience to microaggressions) rather than implementation constraints. It asks about psychological mechanisms, not method performance.
