# Research Plan: Predicting Species-Specific Responses to Climate Change

## Overview
This project analyzes museum collection data to quantify niche shifts in response to climate change. We test the hypothesis that species track their thermal niche by shifting their realized niche centroid over time.

## Methodology

### Niche Centroid Calculation
1. Extract climate values at occurrence coordinates using `raster::extract()`
2. Compute arithmetic mean of climate variables per species per period
3. Calculate Euclidean distance in standardized climate space (Î”N)

### Regional Warming (Î”T)
- Zonal mean temperature change over species occurrence envelope
- **Critical**: Use independent regional climate grid to avoid circularity
- Bounding box defined by min/max lat/lon of species points

### Statistical Analysis
