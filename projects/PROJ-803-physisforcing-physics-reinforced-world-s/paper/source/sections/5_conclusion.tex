\section{Conclusion}

We present PhysisForcing, a training-time framework that improves robotic video generation by
aligning pixel-level trajectories and semantic-level relations on interaction-critical regions.
Across R-Bench, PAI-Bench, and the zero-shot EZS-Bench, it consistently surpasses base models,
vanilla finetuning, and strong open-source, commercial, and robotics-specific baselines, with
PF-Cosmos best overall on all three. It further raises the WorldArena closed-loop success rate
from $16.0\%$ to $24.0\%$ and improves downstream policy success, showing that physical
plausibility yields concrete benefits for embodied intelligence.
