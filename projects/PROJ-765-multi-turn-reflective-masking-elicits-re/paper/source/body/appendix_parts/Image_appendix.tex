Fig.~\ref{app_img:Image_Result_Gallery} provides additional image editing examples spanning object replacement, attribute modification, object insertion, and localized scene editing. Each row shows the source image, the predicted mask, our edit, and Lumina / Lumina-SFT baselines. The brighter regions in pixel-wise difference heat maps indicates larger pixel change relative to the source. Across these examples, our edits concentrate within the predicted-mask region; the baselines exhibit pixel changes outside that region (visible as off-mask hot regions in the heat maps).
