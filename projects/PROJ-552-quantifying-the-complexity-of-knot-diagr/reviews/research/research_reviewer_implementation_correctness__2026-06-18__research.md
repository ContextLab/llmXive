The implementation faithfully follows the design laid out in **spec.md** and **plan.md**, covering all required functional and reproducibility aspects:

### 1. Data Acquisition & Robustness (FR‑001, FR‑008)
* `download/knot_atlas_loader.py` downloads the full Knot Atlas dataset, applies exponential back‑off (1 s → 2 s → 4 s … up to 32 s) and caches partial results after three consecutive failures.  
* Unit‑tests (`tests/integration/test_pipeline.py`) confirm successful download and correct retry behaviour.

### 2. Parsing, Cleaning & Flagging (FR‑002, FR‑009, FR‑010, FR‑011)
* `data/parser.py` extracts crossing number, braid index, hyperbolic volume, and alternating classification, applying the documented tie‑breaking rules (braid word preferred, then lexicographically first DT code).  
* `data/validator.py` generates:
  * `missing_invariant_flags` for absent invariants,
  * `data_quality_flags` for nulls, format violations, or duplicates,
  * `ambiguous_classification_flags` for undefined alternating status.  
* Flags are stored in `processed/knots_validated.csv` and summarized in `docs/reproducibility/data_quality_report.md`.

### 3. Scope Definition & Filtering (FR‑001, FR‑012, SC‑001, SC‑012)
* `filter/hyperbolic_filter.py` removes torus/satellite knots (volume ≤ 0) and records exclusions in `docs/reproducibility/excluded_knots.md`.  
* The final clean dataset (`processed/knots_cleaned.csv`) contains all prime knots with ≤ 13 crossings; a separate validation‑scope document distinguishes the fully validated ≤ 10‑crossing subset.

### 4. Invariant Coverage & Cross‑Check (FR‑013, SC‑008, SC‑013, SC‑014)
* `analysis/invariant_coverage.py` reports coverage statistics in `docs/reproducibility/invariant_coverage.md`.  
* `analysis/hyperbolic_volume_validation.py` cross‑checks hyperbolic volume against KnotInfo, enforcing the ≥ 90 % match rule and documenting source‑independence considerations in `docs/reproducibility/hyperbolic_volume_validation.md`.

* Residual analysis (`analysis/residual_analysis.py`) identifies hyperbolic families deviating ≥ 2 σ; results are documented in `docs/reproducibility/residual_analysis.md`.
