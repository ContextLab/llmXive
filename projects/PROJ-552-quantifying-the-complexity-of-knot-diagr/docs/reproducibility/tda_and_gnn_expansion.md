# Extending Analysis with Topological Data Analysis and Graph Neural Networks

To uncover patterns that are not captured by traditional regression models, we propose
leveraging **persistent homology** on knot diagram representations. By constructing
filtrations over crossing adjacency graphs, we can extract topological signatures
that reflect the underlying complexity of knot projections.

In parallel, **graph neural network (GNN) embeddings** of knot projection graphs
will be trained to learn latent representations that may correlate with knot invariants
beyond linear relationships.
