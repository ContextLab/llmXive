# Established Invariants – Scope Clarification

The crossing number and braid index are classic knot invariants whose basic
relationship (braid index ≤ crossing number) and individual correlations with
hyperbolic volume have been extensively studied in the literature.  This
project does **not** claim to introduce a new invariant or a novel theoretical
transformation; instead it focuses on quantifying knot complexity through
composite metrics and extensive empirical analysis of existing invariants.
