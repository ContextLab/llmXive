# License and Provenance Overview

The codebase for this project is released under the Apache License 2.0, as specified in the top‑level `LICENSE` file.

All data assets are accompanied by provenance records that describe their origin, any transformations applied, and timestamps. These records are stored in `PROVENANCE.md` and the machine‑readable `data/provenance.yaml`.

External datasets, such as the Knot Atlas, are subject to their own licenses. Details can be found in `docs/reproducibility/knot_atlas_data_license.md`.

Please consult these documents to ensure compliance with licensing terms and to understand the data lineage when reproducing or extending the work.
