# Limitations of Established Invariants

The current analysis relies on classic knot invariants such as crossing number and braid
index. While these invariants have well‑studied relationships to hyperbolic volume, they
do not constitute a new theoretical contribution. Consequently, the project does not
introduce a novel invariant or transformation that fundamentally reshapes our
understanding of knot complexity. Future work will explore composite metrics and
potentially new invariants to address this gap.
