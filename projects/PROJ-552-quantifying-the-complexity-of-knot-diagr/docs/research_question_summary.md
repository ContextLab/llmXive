# Research Question and Scope

**Research Question**: *How can the complexity of knot diagrams be quantified in a rigorous, reproducible manner using composite metrics that combine topological invariants, hyperbolic volume, and graph‑theoretic properties?*

**Scope of the Project**:

1. Curate and validate a comprehensive dataset of knot diagrams from the Knot Atlas.
2. Define and implement a suite of invariant‑based and geometric metrics.
3. Develop composite metrics that integrate these individual measures.
4. Provide visualizations and exploratory analyses to interpret metric behavior.
5. Ensure full reproducibility through extensive documentation, validation reports, and open‑source code.
