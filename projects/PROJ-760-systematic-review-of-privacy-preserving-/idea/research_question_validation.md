**Verdict**: pass

The question asks how distinct privacy‑preserving mechanisms (DP, secure aggregation, FHE, hybrids) influence federated learning performance metrics. It concerns a substantive relationship in the domain and does not hinge on the success of any particular algorithmic implementation.

**Verdict**: pass

Both a finding of systematic trade‑offs and a finding of negligible differences would be publishable: the former guides practitioners on expected costs, the latter highlights gaps or robustness in certain mechanisms. The outcome is not predetermined by existing knowledge.

### Question-narrowing check

The research question is framed as a domain‑level inquiry (“how do … affect …”) rather than a constraint on a specific method’s runtime, resources, or implementation details.
