\section{Related Work}
\label{sec:related}
Three lines of work bear on ZPPO. \emph{Knowledge distillation}—whether off-policy, on-policy, or self-distillation~\citep{gou2021knowledge,ko2024distillm,agarwal2024policy,lu2025onpolicydistillation,zelikman2022star,shenfeld2026self}—shares a single training signal, a logit distribution the student must match, which recent analyses show becomes brittle when the student is much smaller than the teacher, inducing memorization and mode-seeking~\citep{kim2026does,li2026rethinking,song2026survey}. \emph{RL post-training} with rule-based rewards on PPO and group-relative variants~\citep{schulman2017proximal,shao2024deepseekmath,yu2025dapo,guo2025deepseek} has advanced reasoning, but leaves the small-student failure mode where every rollout fails and the group advantage is zero~\citep{yu2025dapo} unaddressed; hybrid RL$+$distillation fixes~\citep{xu2025kdrl,xu2026rlkd} target this mode, but route teacher responses into the student's policy gradient through an auxiliary distillation term, which can re-introduce drift. \emph{Prompt replay, curriculum, and hint scaffolding}~\citep{zhan2025exgrpo,zhang2025rlep,zhang2025clpo,zhang2025stephint,zhang2025bread,li2025staying} re-prioritize or scaffold hard questions, but each path compromises a different channel: prompt replay and curriculum~\citep{zhan2025exgrpo,zhang2025rlep,zhang2025clpo} re-expose the same hard prompt without adding any new signal about \emph{why} the previous attempt failed; hint methods~\citep{li2025staying} append a directional cue (without the answer) that the student tends to copy as a shortcut without necessarily internalizing \emph{why} the direction is correct; and prefix methods~\citep{zhang2025stephint,zhang2025bread} insert part of the teacher's response as a prefix into the student's rollout: the student is trained on off-policy trajectories conditioned on teacher response. ZPPO differs from all three by \emph{moving teacher knowledge from the gradient to the prompt} via two reformulations (BCQ and NCQ), so every response is sampled from the student's own policy, with the prompt replay buffer amplifying inside the student's zone of proximal development~\citep{vygotsky1978mind}.
