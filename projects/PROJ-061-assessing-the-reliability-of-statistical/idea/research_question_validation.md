**Verdict**: pass

The question asks about the empirical reliability of a statistical phenomenon (power calculations under real-world conditions) rather than whether a specific method performs well. It targets the gap between theoretical assumptions and practical data conditions.

### Circularity check

**Verdict**: pass

The predictor is theoretical power calculations (derived from sample size, effect size, and alpha assumptions); the predicted variable is actual statistical power achieved in real-world datasets (measured via replication or simulation on empirical data). These are independent sources.
