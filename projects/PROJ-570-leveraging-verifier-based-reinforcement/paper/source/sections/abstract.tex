\section{abstract}
\begin{abstract}
The rapid advancement of diffusion models has catalyzed remarkable progress in the field of text-to-image generation. However, prevalent models such as Flux, SD3.5 and Midjourney, still grapple with issues like model bias, limited text rendering capabilities, and insufficient understanding of Chinese cultural nuances. To address these limitations, we present Seed T2I, an advanced text-to-image model that excels across diverse dimensions, which adeptly manages Chinese and English prompts, supporting bilingual image generation and text rendering. 
We develop a novel data architecture facilitating knowledge integration and a caption system balancing accuracy and richness. A self-developed bilingual large language model integrated as the text encoder, bestows native Chinese support. Glyph-Aligned ByT5 enables flexible character-level rendering, while Scaled Rope adapts image generation to untrained resolutions. Multi-phase post-training optimizations, including SFT and RLHF iterations, further elevate the model's comprehensive capabilities.
Through extensive experimentation, we demonstrate that Seed T2I achieves state-of-the-art performance across multiple dimensions, including prompt-following, aesthetics, text-rendering, structure accuracy. Furthermore, Seed T2I has been optimized through multiple iterations of RLHF to closely align its outputs with human preferences, as revealed by its outstanding ELO score.
\end{abstract}
