\section{Conclusion}

We present $\text{DataClaw}_0$, a structured multimodal data tailoring framework for transforming raw heterogeneous streams into high-quality, schema-aligned training data. 
Extensive experiments show that $\text{DataClaw}_0$ effectively improves structured data generation across diverse multimodal domains. Compared with self-refinement baselines, $\text{DataClaw}_0$ produces substantially higher-quality annotations, and achieves performance competitive with strong proprietary-model annotators under the same data budget. Downstream adaptation results further demonstrate that $\text{DataClaw}_0$-generated data provides useful supervision for targeted model refinement, especially on end-to-end task success metrics.
