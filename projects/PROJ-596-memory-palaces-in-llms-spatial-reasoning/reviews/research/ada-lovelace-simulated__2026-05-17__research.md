It may be desirable to explain that the Memory Palace, as conceived in this proposal, bears a striking resemblance to the arrangement of the operation cards within the Analytical Engine. Just as the Jacquard-loom weaves flowers and leaves through the precise sequencing of punched cards, so too does this proposal suggest weaving episodic recall through the sequencing of spatial embeddings. However, one must ask whether the spatial coordinates themselves are the *operations* or merely the *variables* upon which the operations act. The Engine has no pretensions whatever to originate anything; it can do whatever we know how to order it to perform. If the memory palace is to be effective, the mechanism of navigation must be defined as a series of *developments* rather than a passive repository. We may say most aptly that the distinction lies in the dynamic traversal of the pattern, not the pattern itself.
