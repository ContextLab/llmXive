# Ethics Templates

## Informed Consent Form Template

```
INFORMED CONSENT FORM

Study Title: The Impact of Visual Detail on False Memory Susceptibility

Principal Investigator: [Name]
Institution: [Institution Name]
Contact: [Email] | [Phone]

---------------------------------------------------------------------------

PURPOSE OF THE STUDY
This study investigates how the level of visual detail in images affects the
formation of false memories. Participants will view images, complete a brief
distraction task, and answer questions about what they saw.

PROCEDURES
If you agree to participate, you will:
1. View a series of images on a computer screen
2. Complete a 2-minute arithmetic distraction task
3. Answer 10-15 recognition questions about the images
4. Provide basic demographic information

Total time commitment: Approximately 15 minutes

RISKS AND DISCOMFORTS
The risks associated with this study are minimal. You may experience mild
fatigue from the distraction task. The false memory task is non-invasive and
poses no psychological harm.

BENEFITS
Your participation will contribute to scientific understanding of memory
formation and visual processing. There is no direct benefit to you.

CONFIDENTIALITY
All data will be anonymized. Your responses will be assigned a unique ID
number. No personally identifiable information will be stored with your data.
Data will be kept on password-protected servers accessible only to the
research team.

VOLUNTARY PARTICIPATION
Your participation is entirely voluntary. You may choose not to participate
or may withdraw at any time without penalty. If you withdraw, your data will
be destroyed unless you have given permission for its use.

COMPENSATION
[Describe any compensation, if applicable]

CONTACT INFORMATION
For questions about this study, contact:
[Principal Investigator Name]
[Email]
[Phone]

For questions about your rights as a research participant, contact:
[IRB Office]
[Email]
[Phone]

---------------------------------------------------------------------------

CONSENT

I have read the above information (or it has been read to me) and I agree
to participate in this study. I understand that I may withdraw at any time.

Participant Signature: _________________________ Date: _______________

Researcher Signature: _________________________ Date: _______________
```

## Incident Response Plan

1. **Identification**: Detect and confirm data breach
2. **Containment**: Isolate affected systems
3. **Assessment**: Evaluate scope and impact
4. **Notification**: Notify affected participants within 72 hours
5. **Remediation**: Implement fixes and prevent recurrence
6. **Documentation**: Record all actions taken
7. **Review**: Conduct post-incident analysis
