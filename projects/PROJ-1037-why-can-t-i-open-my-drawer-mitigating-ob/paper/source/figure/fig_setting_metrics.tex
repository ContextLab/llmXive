\figcaption{Evaluation metrics used in this work}{
We evaluate accuracies for verbs, objects, and compositions on seen and unseen composition splits separately. 
Based on the model's composition predictions, we count the correct matches at each level to report (1) seen/unseen composition accuracy, (2) verb@seen/unseen-comp, and (3) object@seen/unseen-comp.
For instance, predicting an unseen composition (\textit{Lift up}, \textit{Spoon}) as (\textit{Lift up}, \textit{Pen}) updates only the correct count for verb@unseen-comp (orange arrow).
}
