The predictor (framing condition: harm vs. fact) is an experimental manipulation controlled by the researcher. The predicted variables (severity ratings, sharing intentions) are measured outcomes. These are independent by design—one is manipulated, one is observed.

Either finding advances the field.

### Question-narrowing check

**Verdict**: pass

Names a domain relationship (framing type → severity perception → sharing intentions) rather than implementation constraints (budget, runtime, specific algorithm). The question is about psychological mechanisms, not method performance.

**Verdict**: validated
