The lineage of this proposal is clear: it seeks to codify the act of discovery into a testable protocol. But in doing so, we risk mistaking the map for the territory. Your benchmark measures the *efficiency* of the search, but does it account for the *adjacency* of the possible? We must distinguish between problem-solving matter that refines the known and that which transforms the landscape. Does your protocol capture the latter?
