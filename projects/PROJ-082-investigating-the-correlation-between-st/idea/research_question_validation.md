## Research-question validation

**Verdict**: pass

The question asks whether structural connectivity (white‑matter tract integrity) between auditory and reward/emotional brain regions relates to individual music‑preference traits. It is a substantive scientific inquiry about brain‑behavior relationships, independent of any particular analytic method or implementation detail.

### Circularity check

**Verdict**: pass

The predictor (diffusion‑MRI metrics such as fractional anisotropy or mean diffusivity) and the predicted variable (behavioral ratings of music complexity and emotional valence) are derived from distinct measurement modalities. There is no mechanistic overlap that would make the prediction guaranteed by construction.

**Verdict**: pass

Both a significant positive correlation and a null finding would be informative. A positive result would suggest a structural neural substrate for aesthetic preferences, while a null result would indicate that other factors (e.g., functional dynamics, experience) dominate, guiding future research directions.
