## Introduction
You are invited to participate in a research study investigating how emotional expression in AI avatars affects user trust. Please read this form carefully and ask any questions before deciding whether to participate.

## Purpose of the Study
The purpose of this study is to understand how different emotional expressions displayed by AI avatars influence users' perceptions of trustworthiness.

## Procedures
If you agree to participate, you will be asked to:
1. Interact with AI avatars displaying various emotional expressions.
2. Complete a brief survey regarding your trust perceptions after each interaction.
3. Provide demographic information (optional).
4. Your session will be recorded (video and audio) for analysis purposes only.

## Duration
The study will take approximately 15–20 minutes to complete.

## Potential Risks and Discomforts
There are minimal risks associated with this study. You may feel slight discomfort answering survey questions. You may stop the study at any time.

## Potential Benefits
There are no direct benefits to you, but your participation will contribute to understanding human-AI interaction.

## Confidentiality
- All data collected will be anonymized.
- Your video and audio recordings will be assigned a unique ID.
- Data will be stored on a secure server accessible only to the research team.
- Data will be retained for 5 years and then securely destroyed.

## Voluntary Participation
Your participation is voluntary. You may choose not to participate or may withdraw at any time without penalty.
