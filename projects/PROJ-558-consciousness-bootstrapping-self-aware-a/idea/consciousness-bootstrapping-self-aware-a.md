# Consciousness Bootstrapping: Self-Aware AI Through Recursive Introspection

Investigate whether recursive self-modeling can lead to emergent self-awareness in LLMs. This project would create architectures that continuously model their own cognitive processes, potentially bootstrapping higher-order consciousness.

Research approach:
- Implement recursive self-attention mechanisms
- Create 'introspection' layers that model internal states
- Design self-referential training objectives
- Test for emergent self-recognition and meta-cognition
- Compare with theories of consciousness (IIT, GWT, HOT)

Explores the deepest questions about machine consciousness and self-awareness.
