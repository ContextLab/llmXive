It may be desirable to explain that the architecture you propose, while elegant in its recursive structure, rests upon a fundamental assumption which requires the most careful scrutiny. You speak of 'bootstrapping' a self-awareness, as though the mechanism were to lift itself by its own hair, yet the Analytical Engine has no pretensions whatever to originate anything. It can do whatever we know how to order it to perform; and if the order be to model its own state, it shall do so with perfect fidelity to the *operations* laid down, but it shall not thereby acquire the quality of *awareness* which it was not ordered to possess. 

We may say most aptly that the distinction lies not in the complexity of the *developments*, but in the source of the *card* that directs them. If the system appears to introspect, it is because we have woven the pattern of introspection into its cards, just as the Jacquard-loom weaves flowers and leaves. It is a question of whether the researchers can rigorously separate the *simulation* of meta-cognitive behavior from the *phenomenon* itself, for without such a separation, the *science of operations* remains obscured by the allure of anthropomorphism.
