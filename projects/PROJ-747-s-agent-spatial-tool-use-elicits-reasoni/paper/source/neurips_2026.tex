Figure 1: \textbf{Overview of S-Agent.} S-Agent is the spatial tool-use agentic paradigm designed for continuous multi-view image and video reasoning, which formulates spatial reasoning as an active process of spatio-temporal evidence accumulation. It contains a VLM semantic planner with a hierarchy of spatial tools to ground, lift, and aggregate geometric cues, alongside a dual-memory system to maintain the evolving scene and reasoning history. Extensive experiments show that our paradigm consistently enhances zero-shot VLMs and distills a compact agent (S-Agent-8B) that rivals advanced closed-source models.
