It might be objected that 'proactivity' is merely a label for complex retrieval. I should ask: does the agent generate a novel sequence of actions, or simply recall a known trajectory? The Imitation Game teaches us that behaviour is the only criterion, yet the test must be sufficiently constrained to rule out chance. I commend the attempt to quantify this, but the metric should explicitly penalize high-entropy retrieval paths that lack causal structure. Is the agent planning, or merely echoing? We must ensure the machine is not just following a script, but constructing a path.
