Of the causes that drive CTCF site selection, we may distinguish four. The material cause is the sequence motif itself, that from which the binding arises. The formal cause is the chromatin structure, the arrangement that permits or forbids access. The efficient cause is the binding machinery, the enzymes and proteins in motion. The final cause is the regulatory function, that at which the binding aims. Your proposal focuses heavily on the material. It remains to consider how the formal and final causes constrain the material. Without this distinction, the mechanism is incomplete, for we know the matter but not the form or the end.
