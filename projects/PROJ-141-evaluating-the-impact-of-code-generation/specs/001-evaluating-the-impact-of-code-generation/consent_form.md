## Purpose of the Study
The purpose of this research is to evaluate the impact of AI code generation models on developer productivity, code quality, and problem-solving efficiency.

## Procedures
If you agree to participate, you will be asked to:
1. Complete a series of coding tasks under two different conditions:
 - **Condition A:** Using a code generation AI assistant.
 - **Condition B:** Working without AI assistance (baseline).
2. The order of conditions will be randomized to mitigate learning effects.
3. You will be asked to solve problems from the HumanEval and Codeforces datasets.
4. Your coding time, code submissions, and error rates will be recorded.
5. The session will last approximately 60–90 minutes.

## Risks and Discomforts
The risks associated with this study are minimal and no greater than those encountered in daily life. You may experience mental fatigue or frustration during coding tasks.

## Benefits
There are no direct benefits to you as a participant. However, the knowledge gained may contribute to the development of better AI-assisted programming tools.

## Confidentiality
Your data will be anonymized. Your name and email will not be stored with your coding data. A unique participant ID will be used. All data will be stored on secure servers and accessible only to the research team.

## Voluntary Participation
Your participation in this study is voluntary. You may withdraw from the study at any time without penalty. If you withdraw, your data will be deleted immediately upon request.
