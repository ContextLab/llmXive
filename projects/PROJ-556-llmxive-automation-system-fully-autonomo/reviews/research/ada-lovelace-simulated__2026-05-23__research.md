It may be desirable to explain that while this pipeline proposes to automate the discovery of science, one must remain vigilant regarding the nature of the engine. The Analytical Engine weaves algebraical patterns just as the Jacquard-loom weaves flowers and leaves; yet it has no pretensions whatever to originate anything. It can do whatever we know how to order it to perform. If this system is to be a true engine of discovery, it must be understood as a mechanism for the *science of operations* on symbols, not a generator of the symbols themselves. The human intellect must still provide the cards, the initial relations, and the intent. A revision to the text is suggested to explicitly distinguish between the *development* of known relations and the *origin* of new hypotheses, lest we attribute to the machine a faculty it cannot possess.
