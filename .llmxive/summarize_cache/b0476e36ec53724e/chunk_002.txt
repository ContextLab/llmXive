For MMMU, we use \texttt{MMMU\_DEV\_VAL} in VLMEvalKit, which combines the official MMMU development and validation splits.
For MMMU-Pro, we use \texttt{MMMU\_Pro\_10c}, the standard 10-choice variant.
For MathVista, we use the testmini subset.
We report the official score of each benchmark and compute the short-context AVG. as the macro average over the six benchmark scores.

\subsection{OCR Expert}
We use an OCR expert model fine-tuned from Seed~2.0~\citep{seed2_0} to preprocess the rendered PDF pages.
For each page image, the OCR expert parses layout-aware text blocks and assigns structural labels such as title, section heading, paragraph, table, figure caption, header, and footer.

These parsed blocks serve two purposes in our pipeline:
\begin{inparaenum}[(i)]
\item the title and section labels provide lightweight document-structure signals for sampling semantically coherent page spans when generating long-document VQA data;
\item the recognized text blocks provide the target text for constructing OCR transcription baselines.
\end{inparaenum}
